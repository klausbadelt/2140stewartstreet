2140 Stewart Street, Santa Monica, CA 90404

Contemporary creative space designed for tech and media professionals.

The Base Zoning District is MUBL - (Mixed Use Boulevard Low) - that allows for a broad range of uses including live work and creative (buyer to verify with City of Santa Monica).

A comfortable 2150 sq.ft. environment with bright airy rooms, wood floors and high cathedral ceilings, embodying the technical infrastructure for cutting edge tech or studio work. The facility has been used for software development, music production, recording, mixing, visual FX creation, editing, and pre/post production for both film and video.

Creative Project Rooms

Six project rooms, of varying sizes with shared equipment racking and a sophisticated facility media wiring scheme. All rooms acoustically treated with soundproof double doors, individual climate control and can be blacked out for video projection. Ideal for software development, music, media and post production.

The Great Room

The Great Room features cathedral ceilings, high book shelves with integrated diffusors, a built-in bass trap and sophisticated media support. Large couches create a relaxing meeting environment. A loft mezzanine provides room for an assistant desk, books and daybed.

Office

A bright office room provides 2 desks and room for 2-6 with a full wall of custom built-in hidden cabinets

Lounge

The lounge boasts a full kitchen, dining counter with bar stools, food and drink fridges and a large wine rack. Seating for meetings or group sessions. A large LED monitor is connected to Roku, AppleTV, Blu-ray player. A 5.1 sound system with hi end Pioneer in-wall speakers.

Bedroom Suites

The facility was built to be the occasional home for artists-in-residence, bands and musicians or late owl software developers. 2 full bathrooms can be assigned exclusively to adjacent bedroom(s). A total of 4 bedrooms can be made available.

Garden Lounge

The garden is a living space to meet and entertain around an outdoor fireplace. Outdoor speakers are connected to the lounge audio system.

Internet

The facility has one of the fastest internet connections of the city. A Synology SAN (5 drives, 10 TB) can be used as file and app server. Wifi is 2.4 and 5GHz (802.11ac).

Parking

Stacked parking for up to 7 cars. Guest parking permits allow street parking.
